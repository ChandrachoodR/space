About

We are a group of intrapreneurs who try to startup within our work space , taking up ideas which can make an impact in the world and creating solutions and designs which we feel as a necessity for humanity . I see myself as a scared entrepreneur who does not want to jump all in to ideas and startup instead choose to work with the organization and build it even though there is an opportunity cost . But its all about choices we make , but either myself and my gang of pirates still try to keep coming up with new ideas and try to build it . Just showcasing a few important ones so that those are not lost .
